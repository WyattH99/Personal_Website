Service

Texas A&M Datathon

During the TAMU Datathon, students spend one weekend in a group of four to solve and develop a solution to a data science problem. Organizations and companies that sponsor the event such as Major League Hacking, Goldman Sachs and Conoco Phillips give the students problems, data sets, and workshops to further their progress. Participating in this event allowed me to develop real-world qualities such as group collaboration, leadership skills, and experience working under pressure.

Aggie Coding Club

Aggie Coding Club (ACC) is a student-led project-oriented organization. ACC brings together the best and brightest students in Computer Science to work together. Projects are student-designed, organized, and managed. These projects can range from mobile apps to web development and building robots. Managing one of these projects allowed me to gain experience that a classroom doesn't provide.

BUILD

BUILD is a student-led and run philanthropist group that constructs Medical Clinics out of shipping containers for third-world countries. BUILD has over 20 Medical Clinics around the world, helping those in need every day while working towards its mission of bringing the student body together. I had the opportunity to participate and become the Design Command Team Leader. Since being in BUILD, I have personally seen ten medical clinics from the start through completion.
